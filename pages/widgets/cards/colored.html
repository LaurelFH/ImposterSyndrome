IMPOSTER SYNDROME Definition

A false and sometimes crippling belief that one's successes are the product of luck or fraud rather than skill.

Imposters often...

Need to be the very best

Have perfectionistic Tendencies (impossible standards)

Fear Failure

Deny their competence and discount praise

Fear distancted from others and guilty about success

"Thank you for the compliment! I really worked hard to develop this app and learned a lot during the process."
"I'm not sure how to do that, would you show me?"
Your work is important and you can make a contribution to your field by being yourself
"I have no idea what I am doing on this task, but I do know how to do this other part of the project..."
"I belong here; I made a mistake but it is a chance to learn and try again"
No more using "only", "just", or other belittling terms; practice your elevator pitch!
